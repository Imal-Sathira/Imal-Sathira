E-commerce App Redesign

UI/UX Design Mobile App 2023

Project Overview

This project involved a complete overhaul of a mobile e-commerce application for a fashion retailer. The goal was to improve conversion rates by simplifying the navigation and streamlining the checkout process.

The redesign resulted in a 35% increase in completed purchases and a 20% reduction in cart abandonment. We focused on creating a more intuitive user flow while maintaining the brand's visual identity.

View Prototype

The Challenge

The existing app had a high bounce rate and low conversion, particularly on mobile devices. Users reported difficulty finding products and a cumbersome checkout process. Our research identified three key pain points:

  • Complex navigation with too many categories
  • Lengthy checkout process with multiple unnecessary steps
  • Poor product discovery and recommendation system

The Solution

We implemented a complete redesign focusing on simplicity and speed. Key improvements included:

  • Simplified navigation with smart categorization
  • One-tap checkout for returning customers
  • Personalized recommendations based on browsing history
  • Improved product visualization with 360° view
  • Enhanced search functionality with filters

The Results

The redesigned app launched to great success, with measurable improvements across all key metrics:

  • 35% increase in completed purchases
  • 20% reduction in cart abandonment
  • 45% faster checkout process
  • 4.8/5 average user rating (up from 3.2)

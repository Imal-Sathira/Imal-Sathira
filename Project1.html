Sport Court Booking App

UI/UX Design Mobile App 2024

Project Overview

This project involved designing a mobile and web-based sports court booking application to simplify the process of finding and reserving local sports venues. The goal was to enhance user convenience and improve court utilization by offering real-time availability, easy booking, and digital payments.

View Prototype

The Challenge

The sports court booking process was largely offline or scattered across inconsistent platforms, leading to user frustration and underutilized facilities. Our initial research revealed several key pain points that hindered both players and venue managers:

  • Lack of centralized platform to discover and book courts across different sports and locations
  • Manual and outdated booking methods, often requiring phone calls or in-person visits
  • Limited visibility into real-time availability, causing confusion and double bookings
  • No digital payment options, leading to missed revenue and inconvenience for users

The Solution

We designed a streamlined, intuitive booking experience focused on speed, clarity, and usability across both mobile and web platforms. Key improvements included:

  • Clean, location-based layout with quick access to popular sports and nearby venues
  • Real-time availability, facility photos, amenities, pricing, and user reviews
  • Simplified 3-step flow: select date/time → choose court → confirm & pay
  • Powerful admin tools for court owners to manage availability, pricing, and bookings
  • Smart filters by sport type, distance, rating, and time slots to enhance discoverability
  • Seamless online payments with support for local and international gateways

The Results

The new sports court booking app launched with strong user adoption and significant improvements across core performance metrics:

  • 40% increase in successful court bookings
  • 60% reduction in booking-related support calls
  • 2.5x growth in venue registrations within the first 3 months
  • 50% Average booking time reduced by 50%
